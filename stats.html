Pollution Statistics

Pollution affects millions globally. Here are some statistics on its impact:

  • Over 7 million deaths each year are linked to air pollution.
  • 80% of urban areas have pollution levels above WHO standards.
  • More than 70% of industrial waste is dumped untreated into water sources.
